Full Description  
———————————————————————  
Founded in 2023, Augusta Pest Control is committed to protecting local homes and businesses from unwanted pests with effective, eco-conscious solutions. We understand that pest problems can be stressful, which is why we offer fast response times, honest pricing, and a service guarantee you can count on. Our skilled team handles a wide range of infestations, including termites, bed bugs, stinging insects, rodents, ants, cockroaches, raccoons, and mosquitoes.

Every treatment plan is tailored to your specific needs, ensuring maximum effectiveness while keeping your family, pets, and environment safe. We combine proven pest control methods with advanced technology to target infestations at the source and prevent them from returning.

Serving the Augusta, GA community and nearby areas, we are proud to be a locally owned business that values customer satisfaction above all else. Whether you require a one-time extermination or ongoing maintenance, Augusta Pest Control delivers dependable results with professionalism and care. Contact us today for a free consultation and let us help you reclaim your space from pests—quickly, safely, and affordably.
