10 Common Roofing Mistakes Kalamazoo Homeowners Make (And Expert Solutions)

By Mike Johnson, Senior Roofing Expert at Kalamazoo Roofing Contractors 10/8/2023
Discover the most common roofing mistakes that Kalamazoo homeowners make, from skipping regular inspections to attempting DIY repairs without proper knowledge. Our local roofing experts share essential tips to protect your roof investment and avoid costly repairs. Learn about proper maintenance, the importance of professional installation, and why choosing the right contractor matters in Michigan's challenging climate.

As experienced roofing contractors serving Kalamazoo and surrounding areas like Parchment and Oshtemo Charter Township, we've seen our fair share of roofing mishaps. Here's your guide to avoiding costly mistakes and protecting your home's most important asset.

1. Skipping Regular Roof Inspections

Many homeowners wait until they spot a leak to schedule a professional roof inspection. By then, the damage is often extensive and costly. We recommend bi-annual inspections, especially after Michigan's harsh winters.

2. DIY Repairs Without Proper Knowledge

While DIY projects can save money, roofing requires specialized expertise. Improper repairs often lead to emergency roof services and more expensive fixes down the line.

Common DIY Mistakes Include:

  • Incorrect shingle placement
  • Poor flashing installation
  • Improper nail placement
  • Inadequate ventilation solutions

3. Ignoring Ventilation Issues

Proper attic ventilation is crucial in Kalamazoo's varying climate. Poor ventilation leads to:

  • Ice dam formation in winter
  • Premature shingle aging
  • Higher energy bills
  • Mold growth

4. Layering New Shingles Over Old Ones

While technically allowed in some cases, layering shingles can hide underlying damage and reduce your roof's lifespan. Learn more about proper installation in our guide to roofing materials and durability.

5. Neglecting Proper Documentation

Many Kalamazoo homeowners don't keep detailed records of roof repairs and warranties. Read our article on understanding roof warranties to protect your investment.

6. Poor Contractor Selection

Choosing an unqualified contractor can lead to substandard work and voided warranties. Always verify:

  • Local licensing and insurance
  • References and reviews
  • Written estimates
  • Warranty terms

7. Ignoring Gutter Maintenance

Clogged gutters cause water damage and foundation issues, especially in areas like Richland with heavy tree coverage.

8. Delaying Necessary Repairs

Small issues quickly become major problems. Schedule prompt repairs when you notice:

  • Missing or damaged shingles
  • Water stains on ceilings
  • Granules in gutters
  • Daylight through roof boards
